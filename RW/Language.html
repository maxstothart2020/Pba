Language

The author uses very simple sentences. All the writing is small and centered in a white page. and repeatedly uses the words Tok Tok Tok. I think Tok Tok Tok means the ticking sound you make when your disagreeing with someone/thing because Cicada is a Cicada he can’t make the click like we can. It was also the author’s favourite band. No page has more than 4 lines of text. I think this is because the author wants the story to come from the perspective of Cicada.
